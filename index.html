Benefits

Clapping dugs is obviously beneficial for both the dug and the human. That is, if the dogs like to be petted. Luckily, ours do, though Nomi more so than Magnus. The upside of virtually sending cuddles to a dog is that we can make sure they’re up for it before we deliver your pets to them.

Just make sure to always ask before approaching a dog irl. Pet carefully and pay attention to the dogs’ reaction and body language.
